About Us

We believe in empowering young minds to excel in the world of technology.

Our aim is to get patform where youth in Burundi will access learning ressources. The website offers free tech resources, training, and mentorship to bridge the digital skills gap. Our mission is rooted in the belief that technology can uplift communities, boost economic growth, and amplify youth voices as it will help youths to get access to the opportunities around the world. By providing free tech resources, training, and mentorship to empower Burundian youth. Together, we will spark a technology-driven revolution, building a brighter future for Burundi.

What we expext

We believe that by using the website which offers technology workshops, mentorship programs, and access to digital resources, we will significantly improve the digital literacy in youth.
